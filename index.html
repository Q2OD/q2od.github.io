Booking Guidelines

Everything you need to know before you book. No surprises, no confusion.

!

Most Important Rule

Booking is for my time filming/photographing and editing, not guaranteed highlights or photo counts.

You're paying for my time at the event and editing work—not a specific number of clips or photos. I'll capture what's available during the event.

1

Free Tier

First-time clients only

  • • One free introductory highlight reel may be offered
  • • Free highlights are up to 30 seconds
  • • Best plays only
  • • One delivery
  • • No revisions unless upgrading to paid package
  • • Availability is limited—paid clients are prioritized
2

Paid Service

Professional coverage

  • • Video: $25 single-game or $40 two-game bundle
  • • Photography: $20 Express / $35 Pro / $50 Premium
  • • Photo packages include edited action shots, candids, and portrait-style photos
  • • Video or photo: focus is on booked athlete, not entire team
  • • Final video length and photo counts are based on available content captured
3

Payment

  • • Payment is split: 50% due on the day of the shoot, 50% due before delivery
  • • Delivery begins after full payment is received
  • • Rush delivery and add-ons cost extra
4

Travel

  • • Free travel within 5 miles of Oviedo
  • • +$5 for every additional 5 miles outside that area (rounded up)
5

Game Entry / Tickets

  • • If a game requires a ticket for entry, the client is responsible for providing or covering the ticket
  • • Press or school passes are not guaranteed to work at every venue
6

Playing Time / Footage

Important — Read Carefully

  • • Playing time is not guaranteed
  • • If an athlete receives little or no playing time and usable clips are limited or unavailable, there is no refund
  • • The highlight reel is based on whatever usable footage is available from the event
  • • Lack of playing time does not void payment or booking

FAQ

How long does it take?

Usually 2–4 days depending on schedule. Rush delivery is available for +$10.

Can I pick the song?

Yes. The choice is yours. Clean versions are preferred for high school content.

Do you cover multiple athletes?

I can, but each athlete needs their own booking. One price does not cover the whole team or roster. This applies to both video and photography.

What if I want changes?

Small tweaks are fine. Full re-edits or major style changes count as a re-edit add-on.

What if the athlete doesn't play much?

The highlight reel uses whatever usable footage is available. Limited playing time doesn't void the booking or payment.

How do I pay?

50% due on shoot day, 50% before delivery. Delivery begins after full payment.

What photo formats do I get?

You get edited JPGs optimized for social media (1080px, perfect for Instagram/Twitter). Print-ready high-resolution files are available as a +$10 add-on.

Can I get both video and photos?

Absolutely! Combo packages save you $5. I capture both during the same event—you get a highlight reel and a full photo set.

How many photos will I get?

Express ($20): 15+ action shots. Pro ($35): 30+ photos (action, candids, portraits). Premium ($50): 50+ photos with full event coverage including pre/post-game moments.

Do you take team photos?

I focus on individual athlete coverage (action, candids, portraits). If you need team/group shots, let me know in advance—it may require the Premium package depending on the request.
