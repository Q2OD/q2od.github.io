Booking Guidelines

No surprises. Everything you need to know before booking.

!

Most Important Rule

Booking is for my time filming/photographing and editing — not a guaranteed number of clips or photos.

You're paying for my time at the event and editing work. I capture what's available during the shoot.

1

Free Tier

First-time clients only

  • •One free 30-second highlight reel
  • •Best plays only · one delivery · watermarked
  • •No revisions unless upgrading to paid
  • •Paid clients are always prioritized
2

Paid Service

  • •Video: $65 Game Highlight / $125 Feature Edit
  • •Photo: $40 Essentials / $75 Collection / $120 Full Story
  • •Focus is on the booked subject — not full team/crowd
  • •Final deliverables are based on available content captured
3

Payment

  • •Payment is split: 50% due on shoot day, 50% before delivery
  • •Delivery begins after full payment is received
  • •Rush delivery and add-ons cost extra
4

Entry / Access

  • •If a venue requires a ticket for entry, the client is responsible for providing or covering it
  • •Press or credential passes are not guaranteed at every venue
5

Footage Availability

Important — Sports bookings

  • •Playing time is not guaranteed
  • •Limited footage does not void the booking or payment
  • •The highlight reel is based on whatever usable footage is available
  • •Lack of playing time does not void payment or booking

FAQ

How long does turnaround take?

Turnaround depends on the project scope and my current availability — typically a few days to a week. Rush delivery (48 hours) is available for +$35.

What areas do you serve?

Based in Oviedo, FL. I cover Central Florida. DM or contact me if you're unsure whether your location works.

Do you do events and commercial work?

Yes. Concerts, parties, brand content, promo videos, and documentary shorts. These are custom-quoted — contact me to discuss your project and I'll put together a quote.

Can I pick the song for my highlight?

Yes. The choice is yours. Clean versions are preferred for high school content.

What if I want changes?

Small tweaks are fine. Full re-edits or major style changes count as a revision add-on (+$25).

How do I pay?

50% due on shoot day, 50% before delivery. Delivery begins after full payment is received.

What formats do I get?

Video: vertical + horizontal exports, platform-ready. Photos: high-resolution downloads included on Collection and above. Print-ready files included on Full Game Story and Premium Event tiers.

What if the athlete doesn't play much?

Deliverables are based on available footage. Limited playing time does not void the booking or payment.

Can I get both video and photos?

Yes — combo packages cover both from the same event. Save $5 vs. booking separately.

How do I book events or commercial work?

Fill out the contact form or DM me on Instagram. Tell me about the project and I'll put together a custom quote based on scope and deliverables.
