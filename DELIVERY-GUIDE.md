# Caleb Media — Internal Delivery Guide

What you need to collect from the client, what you need to deliver, and in what format. Use this as a checklist before every job.

---

## Before ANY Job — Info to Collect First

Regardless of service type, always confirm these before showing up:

- [ ] Client full name
- [ ] Contact method (Instagram, phone, email)
- [ ] Event date, time, and exact location (address)
- [ ] Payment confirmed: 50% received before or on shoot day
- [ ] Any special requests or notes discussed and documented
